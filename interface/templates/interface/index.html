Unique Design

Accord carries a consistent design across all Android and iOS devices. This allows you to to set up your app only once and still reach all of your church members where they are.

Church Branding

Every church has a unique look and feel. Accord allows you to customize the colors, logos, and images in your app whenever you want.

Any time support

Because the content of the app is completely in your hands, support is always available to answer any questions you may have.
